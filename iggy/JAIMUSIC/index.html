INTRODUCTION


JANKOVSKY (Igor Jankovski, also known as Iggy Jai) is an Estonian music producer, musician, former drummer for Ebony Bones. He worked and studied in London, UK from 2010-2020. He's a graduate of Abbey Road Institute in London (Advanced music production and sound engineering) and University of Tallinn BA (Orchestral conducting). â€‹

Not only did he learn the keys to a successful song in any genre be it pop, indie, hip hop or an entire movie score, he also gained priceless contacts reaching far and wide within the industry who are responsible for artists such as 'The Verve', 'U2', 'Sam Smith', 'The Cult', 'Dido'. JANKOVSKY worked with singers from 'Let it Shine', 'X Factor UK', 'BBC Radio 1' as well as companies such as VICE and Red Bull Academy. In addition to his production work, he has toured the world and performed on stages in USA, China, Russia, Europe, Australia and New Zealand, headlining venues such as Le Trianon (FRA), Berghain (GER), KOKO (UK), La Machine (FRA), Splore (NZ).

Up until recently JANKOVSKY has been working with bespoke artists by a private appointment only but now he's looking to expand and provide services to the broader public. His mission is to help up and coming artists to get the best out of their performance by crafting a unique and definitive sound for their songs, as well as presenting their finished product to record labels.
